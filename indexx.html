WHY CUSTOMERS LEAVE ORGANISATION

Research has made us to understand that:

  1. 1% dies: maybe one percent of your customer’s dies you can’t determine if a customer dies or not.
  2. 3% move away: these can happen if one of your loyal customers migrate to another country which you can’t stop them.
  3. 9% leave for competitive reason: which is normal because as an organization you are not cut out to serve everybody.
  4. 9% leave for competitive reason: which is normal because as an organization you are not cut out to serve everybody.
  5. 14% are dissatisfied because of the product: at this point you need to be sensitive and do something about it.
  6. 68% go elsewhere because of the poor way they were treated by the employee of the company

The last two issues are preventable especially the 68% is very avoidable and they carry the larger percent, imagine you loosing 82% of your customers to issues that are within your control then there is problem. It is very important that you take care of issues around this last two percent. Management theory says there are three things that will makes an organization works which are:

  1. Process
  2. Technology
  3. The people

Among these 3 management theory the most critical of it allis the people because if you have the best process people can come and destroy the process, if you implement the best technology you will still rely on people to enable or disable the technology. That is why organization need to concentrate more on the people side of the organization.

WHAT IS EXCELLENT SERVICE

Customer perception minus customer expectation. The battle for repeat business is critical to long-term success in today’s intensely competitive market place. Customers service is not just a competitive edge service is the new standard by which customers judge performance. Having these at the back of your mind you will not render anything short of quality excellent service to your customers meaning you have to serve your customer with a smile on your face even when things don’t go right with you. It means going the extra mile when someone is in need of your help, it means caring for your client and co-worker. If all you do is to care for your client that is hypocrisy. Excellent serviceisall about surprising a customer with a random act of kindness. It means following up with a courtesy phone call after you’ve put things right. Service is better define by meeting the functions such as:

  1. It helps you maintain old customers
  2. It attract new customers
  3. Leaving all customers with an impression of inducement to do business with the organization again and again.
